In the late '80s and early '90s, over forty percent of professionals in computer science woman, and the field of computer science was considered "appropriate" for women. However, as the computer industry began to advance and become increasingly successful, men took over the industry and disuaded women from entering the field. Today, the number of women professionals in computer sciences continues to decrease.
